# Uploading workflows to OMERO

J2O will automatically offer you all .jip files and RO-Crates that are accessible by your OMERO groups. If you have none available, you can upload files as attachments to any of your projects or datasets. To do so, select a dataset or project and <b>go to General → Attachments in the right panel.</b> Click the <b>+</b> to attach a file. To ensure compatibility, be sure to follow the [*Preparing JIPipe workflows* section](WorkflowDesign.topic).

> After uploading new data, you need to refresh the page to update its contents.
